ЁЯУШ Class 10 Science - Chapter 1: Life Processes

Q1. What are life processes?

Life processes are basic activities like nutrition, respiration, transportation, and excretion that living organisms perform to stay alive.

Q2. What is nutrition? Name its types.

Nutrition is the process of taking in and using food. It is of two types: Autotrophic (plants) and Heterotrophic (animals).

Q3. Why is respiration important?

Respiration helps in breaking down food to release energy necessary for all life activities.
